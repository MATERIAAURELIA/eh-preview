◦  About Us

Pioneers and Explorers in therapeutic sound

Based in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada, Earth Harmony Sound & Wellness has been operating under different names for over 20 years. Serving diverse populations, we are pioneers in the field of using sound, rhythm and participatory music making for therapy and enhanced well-being.

Marcus Fung blends his training as a classical and jazz musician, music producer, alternative healing and expressive arts therapy practitioner to facilitate inclusive, and transformative experiences.

◦  What We Offer

Our services

Deep Planetary Resonance Sound Bath

Our premium offering supports better sleep, emotional processing, nervous system regulation and relaxation — a full frequency spectrum of vibration using professional, deeply resonating instruments.

Singing Bowl Meditation

Quiet the mind and promote brain wave shifts into deep relaxation. The harmonies of a beautiful crystal singing bowl set are a great introduction to sound therapy — ideal for lunch hours or staff retreats.

Wellness Drumming Circles & Team Building

With over two decades of drum circle facilitation expertise, we offer joyful, interactive, energizing circles — a synergizing, light-hearted team building for your corporate or non-profit event.

The Rhythm Cradle — A Team Building Experience

A participant-driven, facilitator-led therapeutic sound experience focused on communication, trust, reciprocity, and sharing feedback toward a sense of accomplishment and team wellbeing.

Custom Corporate Wellness Packages

Talk to us about your event’s desired outcomes and we’ll customize a package — combining any of the above with mindfulness, yoga breathing and other wellness components.

Private Small Group Bookings

Have a small team or group of friends you’d like to invite to a sound bath? Our living room sessions are the perfect combination of cozy, intimate, and professional — accommodates up to 8 people.
